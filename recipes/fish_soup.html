Creamy Fish Soup

Soup the is filled with potatos and vegetables

The fish will be Lohta!

Ingredients

  • Potatos
  • Cream
  • Vegetables(of your choise)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. First cut the potatos
  2. Then warm the water
  3. Add the vegetables
  4. Add the fish
  5. Last add the cream and spices
